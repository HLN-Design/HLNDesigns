Eras Tour.

It's the end of an era. This project marks the conclusion of Taylor Swift's record-breaking 21-month Eras Tour. To capture the magnitude and cultural impact of the event, I developed a visual language that evolves from the vibrant aesthetic of the tour into a darker, more sophisticated tone aligned with the release of her new album.

Conceived as a visual homage to the artist, the design incorporates iconic elements of the Swift brand, including the tour poster, typography, and fan-centric motifs like friendship bracelets, blending them seamlessly with high-energy concert imagery. The dynamic camera movements guides the viewer through to key statistics while preserving the visual richness of the source material while maintaining narrative flow.

This project later was shared on Tiktok and accumulated 387,700 views.

"So many people mentioned to me how great it looked - including elements from Taylor's tour that I hadn't even flagged. Very creative and visually stunning!!" - Supervising Producer
