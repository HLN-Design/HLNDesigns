Iran Strike.

This explainer covered the US military strikes on Iran's nuclear infrastructure. The narrative includes breaking down the deployment of "bunker buster" munitions against the Fordow nuclear fortress and explaining the timeline and execution of the attack.

I developed a Military Heads-Up Display (HUD) aesthetic using high-contrast blue wireframes and digital grid overlays to mimic a command center briefing. Using GeoLayers 3 in After Effects, I generated a terrain map of Iran to visualize the flight paths taken by B-2 bombers and Tomahawk missiles.

The project aired the same day on 9News, reaching an audience of 1.98 million viewers, and later accumulated an additional 420,000 views on the 9News YouTube channel.
