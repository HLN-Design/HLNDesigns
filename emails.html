Parliamentary Leak.

This exclusive report uncovered a major breach of protocol, where 100,000+ parliamentary emails was handed to HWL Ebsworth, a law firm previously compromised by a Russian hack.

To visualise this bureaucratic cyber-risk, I developed a clean, Desktop Simulation aesthetic. Using a laptop model and animated UI interfaces, I am able showcase the impact of the breach in a unique way. This graphic simplified a complex IT scandal, visually connecting a dry administrative decision to a tangible real-world cyber threat for the audience.
