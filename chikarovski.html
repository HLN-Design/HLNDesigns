Chikarovski Drug Sentencing.

Mark Chikarovski, son of a former NSW Liberal leader, was found guilty for operating a sophisticated dark web drug syndicate. This explainer showcases his operations and how he got caught.

To bring the "dark web" to the screen, I developed a high-tech look, utalising glowing typography and chromatic aberration. Key digital evidence was visualised by turning abstract data into tangible objects.

The project aired, reaching an audience of 1.57 million viewers, and later accumulated an additional 33,700 views on social media.
