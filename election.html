Federal Election 2025.

As the Federal Election 2025 approached, this explainer mapped the complex political battleground of the nation. The objective was to transform raw polling data and geographic clusters into a tangible roadmap for the major parties.

Leveraging GeoLayers in After Effects to generate a data-accurate national map, ensuring electorates were represented in their proper geographic context. By applying a holographic aesthetic with distinct colour-coding, I translated dense electoral data into a dynamic visual strategy that allowed the audience to track the shifting landscape.

On Election night this explainer reached an audience of 1.78 million viewers.
