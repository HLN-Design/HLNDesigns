Moriarty Appointment.

This project profiles the strategic appointment of Greg Moriarty as Australia's next Ambassador to the United States. The objective was to provide a comprehensive overview of his diplomatic history while visualising the geopolitical weight of this transition.

By layering government documents, press releases, & archival footage with tactile paper textures, I am able to create a sense of depth and history. The colour palette, reinforces the nature of international diplomacy while maintaining a clean, authoritative look.

This project was a last-minute addition to the bulletin and was critical to the broadcast, ultimately comprising nearly half of the segment's duration. I designed & delivered the project in just four hours, ensuring it was communicated clearly in time for the top slot to broadcast audience of 2.1 million.
