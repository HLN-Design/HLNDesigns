Warner Brothers Acquisition.

Visualising Netflix's $108 billion acquisition of Warner Brothers, focusing on the consolidation of media power, merging the world's number one streaming service with one of Hollywood's most iconic movie studios.

To illustrate the collision of media giants, I wanted it to have a Netflix user interface look. Using a clean, dark-mode aesthetic, I utilised kinetic typography and sliding UI panels to break down the data. This visual approach transformed complex financial figures into a relatable user experience, showing exactly how the new entity would dominate the entertainment landscape.

This graphic simplified a massive corporate merger, using familiar visual language to make the business implications immediately understood by a general audience.
